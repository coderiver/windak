Windak Provides Cost-Effective Automated Packaging Solutions For The Wire & Cabling Industries

Founded in 1993, in Stockholm Sweden, Windak maintains offices in Hickory, North Carolina, Sydney, Australia and Tallinn, Estonia.

Windak offers a wide range of both standard and customized products. Material handling solutions include palletizing and pallet handling equipment. Packaging solutions include coiling and spooling equipment for wire and cable, tubing and other flexible products..
